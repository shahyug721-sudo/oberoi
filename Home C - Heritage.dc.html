THE HOUSE

A Mumbai house of hardware

Oberoi Enterprise works from Gurunanak Udyog Bhavan in Bhandup — manufacturers and importers of exclusive hardware fittings. Fittings are made to our own patterns, exclusive ranges are brought in from abroad, and everything leaves the house under one mark.

Every fitting in the range carries a code. Quote it with a quantity and the price comes back the same way — hardware stores, furniture makers, glass fabricators and builders order by the piece or by the container, in stainless steel, antique brass, chrome, black and rose gold.
